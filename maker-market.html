Port Henry Waterfront Farmers Market

The 2024 Port Henry Waterfront Farmers Market!

Experience the vibrant Port Henry Waterfront Farmers Market, an unforgettable summer event presented by the Moriah Chamber of Commerce and Bridgeview Harbour Marina.

Returning for its second year the Port Henry Waterfront Farmer's Market will be held every Friday from Father's Day through Labor Day, from 5 pm to 8 pm, the breathtaking Bridgeview Harbour Marina Waterfront transforms into a bustling marketplace. Indulge in a wide variety of locally sourced produce, artisanal foods, and unique handcrafted goods, while also enjoying live music, food trucks, and fun lawn games.

As the sun dips below the horizon, witness the stunning sunset over Lake Champlain, creating an ambiance that perfectly complements the festive mood. The Port Henry Waterfront Farmers Market not only supports local businesses but also offers an enchanting evening full of community spirit, sustainable living, and unforgettable views. Join us, and make your Fridays vibrant and farm-fresh!

If you are interested in participating in this year's market as a vendor, performing musician, or volunteer, please visit the market website at www.porthenrymarket.org or reach out to info@porthenrymarket.org
